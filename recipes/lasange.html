lasagne

Description

Choose hot or sweet Italian sausage for this classic lasagna recipe. It takes some time to make, but it feeds a crowd, and the leftovers are even better the next day!

This recipe is comfort food at its finest, layered with ground beef, Italian sausage, and cheese.

ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook ground beef and sausage in a large pot over medium heat; drain. Stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms, garlic salt, oregano, thyme, and basil; season with salt and black pepper. Simmer 30 minutes.
